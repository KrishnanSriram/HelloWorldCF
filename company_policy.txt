ACME Corporation - Employee Policy Document

VACATION POLICY
- Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year.
- Vacation days accrue at 1.25 days per month.
- Unused vacation days can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days.
- Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
- Remote work must be approved by the direct manager.
- Core hours are 10am to 3pm in the employee's local timezone.
- All remote employees must attend in-person team meetings quarterly.

EXPENSE POLICY
- Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to $75 per day.
- Flight bookings must be economy class for trips under 6 hours.
- All expenses over $500 require VP approval before being incurred.
- Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of the expense.

PERFORMANCE REVIEW POLICY
- Reviews are conducted twice a year, in June and December.
- Employees must complete a self-assessment 2 weeks before the review.
- Salary adjustments take effect the first of the month after review.
